# Revenue Models — Research

## 1. Dam & Irrigation Revenue ("Water QCA")

| Service | Payer | Model |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Dam safety compliance-as-a-service | State govts / dam operators | Annual SaaS per dam |
| Canal distribution optimization | Irrigation departments | Per-command-area subscription |
| Flood forecasting (7-day) | State disaster mgmt agencies | Per-basin subscription |
| Water theft detection | Irrigation depts / WUAs | Revenue share on recovered water |
| Inter-state water audit | CWC / tribunals | Project-based contracts |
| Crop advisory | Farmer cooperatives / agri-input companies | Freemium / B2B2C |

## 5. Cross-Sell Opportunity

The same platform and client base can serve:
- Energy compliance (RE forecasting & scheduling)
- Environmental compliance (CEMS/OCEMS, ETS)
- Water compliance (dam safety, irrigation)

Industrial clients often need all three simultaneously. Hydropower dams need both water forecasting and energy scheduling.

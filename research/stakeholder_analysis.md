## Engagement Strategy: How a Startup Gets In

### Without Manikaran's Brand, We Use:

1. **Academic partnership as the door opener**
   - VNIT Nagpur has existing relationships with VIDC and CWC
   - "Joint research project with VNIT" gets us site access without formal procurement
   - Publications + conference presentations build credibility organically

2. **The regulatory mandate as the sales argument**
   - We don't sell technology — we sell compliance
   - "The Dam Safety Act requires this. You currently can't do it. We make it affordable."
   - VIDC leadership is under political pressure to show compliance

3. **A working demo as proof**
   - Before any meeting, build a prototype on India-WRIS open data
   - Show VIDC engineers: "This is what your dam data looks like on our platform"
   - Tangible demo > slide deck for government audiences

4. **Encardio Rite as a channel partner**
   - They already sell to every major dam operator
   - They need a software/AI layer they can't build
   - "We come recommended by your existing sensor vendor"

5. **Domain advisor as an insider**
   - A retired CWC Chief Engineer or VIDC Superintending Engineer
   - Opens doors that cold outreach never will
   - Provides regulatory interpretation and credibility

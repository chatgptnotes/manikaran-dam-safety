# Executive Summary

## The Problem

India has **5,334 large dams**, over 1,100 of which exceed their 50-year design life. The **Dam Safety Act 2021** now mandates real-time instrumentation and compliance reporting — yet fewer than 15% of dams have adequate monitoring. Most state dam operators lack the technical capacity and digital infrastructure to comply. Non-compliance carries penalties including imprisonment.

No company in India currently offers affordable, end-to-end Dam Safety Act compliance as a service.

## Our Solution

We build an **affordable IoT monitoring + AI compliance platform** for dams:

1. **Install low-cost sensors** (piezometers, crack meters, tiltmeters) on the dam
2. **Stream data to a cloud dashboard** via LoRaWAN in real-time
3. **Detect anomalies** automatically (threshold alerts, trend analysis, ML over time)
4. **Generate compliance reports** in NDSA/SDSO-mandated formats with one click

The dam operator gets continuous monitoring and automated compliance. We get recurring SaaS revenue per dam.

## Starting Point: One Dam, One Section

We begin with a **2 km critical section of Gosikhurd Dam** (Bhandara, Maharashtra):

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Dam | Gosikhurd — 11.35 km earthen, INR 25,972 Cr invested, only 14% irrigated |
| Initial scope | 20 sensors on 2 km section |
| Sensor cost | ~INR 15 lakh |
| Cloud cost | ~INR 2 lakh/year |
| Timeline | 6 months to first data, 12 months to first compliance report |

**Why Gosikhurd:** National visibility (PM-level attention), existing Encardio Rite sensors to build on, highest-risk earthen dam type, clear ROI story for irrigation optimization.

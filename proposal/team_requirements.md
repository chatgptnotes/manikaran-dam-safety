# Team Requirements — Lean Startup Phasing

## Phase 0: Founding Team (2-3 people + 1 advisor)

| Role | Who | Compensation | Key Requirement |
|------|-----|-------------|-----------------|
| **CEO / Founder** | You | Equity-heavy, INR 0-6 LPA | Business development, fundraising, government relationships |
| **CTO / Co-founder** | Hire or find | Equity-heavy, INR 0-12 LPA | IoT + ML + full-stack; ideally some hydrology/infrastructure exposure |
| **Domain Advisor** | Ex-CWC or retired dam engineer | INR 2-4 LPA part-time retainer | Credibility, introductions, regulatory interpretation, sensor selection |

**Finding a CTO/co-founder:** Look in IoT/embedded communities (Bangalore, Pune, Hyderabad), IIT alumni networks (especially Civil + CS), or ex-Encardio-Rite engineers who want to build on the software side.

## Phase 1: MVP Team (5-6 people)

| Role | Count | CTC (INR LPA) | Key Skills |
|------|-------|---------------|-----------|
| CEO / Founder | 1 | 6-10 | BD, fundraising, stakeholder management |
| CTO | 1 | 12-18 | IoT architecture, ML basics, system design |
| IoT / Embedded Engineer | 1 | 8-12 | LoRaWAN, Modbus RTU, data logger config, field deployment |
| Full-Stack Developer | 1 | 10-15 | React + Python/FastAPI, real-time dashboards, REST APIs |
| Field Technician | 1 | 4-6 | ITI/Diploma; Nagpur-based; sensor installation, solar panel maint. |
| Domain Advisor | 1 | 2-4 (part-time) | Dam safety, instrumentation interpretation, regulatory context |
| **Total** | **6** | **42-65 LPA** | |

**Where people work:**
- CEO: Flexible (Delhi/Nagpur/remote, travel heavy)
- CTO: Can be remote (Bangalore/Pune) with monthly Nagpur visits
- IoT Engineer: Nagpur (needs to be at dam site frequently)
- Developer: Remote OK
- Field Tech: Nagpur (full-time at dam)
- Advisor: Part-time, remote + occasional site visits

## Key External Partners (In Lieu of In-House)

Instead of hiring everyone, partner strategically:

| Partner | Role | Why Partner |
|---------|------|------------|
| **Encardio Rite** (Lucknow) | Sensor supply, installation supervision, calibration | They have 250+ dam projects; saves us from building field ops from scratch |
| **VNIT Nagpur** | Research credibility, graduate students for data analysis | Academic access + publications at low cost |
| **Skylark Drones** (Bengaluru) | Quarterly drone surveys (if needed) | Per-survey basis; no full-time hire needed |
| **SkyGeo / DeepInSAR** | InSAR satellite monitoring | Service contract; deep niche expertise |
| **Freelance / Agency** | UI/UX design, branding, pitch deck | One-time projects |

---

## Hiring Principles

1. **Generalists over specialists** at this stage — everyone wears multiple hats
2. **Equity over cash** for co-founders — preserve cash runway
3. **Remote-first** where possible — talent pool is limited in Nagpur for ML/software
4. **One person in Nagpur minimum** — field presence at the dam is non-negotiable
5. **Don't hire ahead of need** — each Phase has a decision gate before expanding

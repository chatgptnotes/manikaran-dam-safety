# Expected Outcomes & ROI

## 1. MVP Outcomes (20 sensors, 2 km section, 12 months)

| Outcome | What It Proves |
|---------|---------------|
| Real-time sensor data on dashboard | Technical viability; continuous monitoring works |
| First automated compliance report | We can generate what NDSA/SDSO requires |
| Monsoon season data captured | Foundation for ML model training |
| VIDC engineers use the dashboard | Product-market fit signal |
| At least 1 anomaly detected or threshold alert triggered | Platform adds value over manual inspection |

## 2. Phase 2 Outcomes (Full Gosikhurd, 18 months)

| Outcome | Baseline (Current) | Target | Impact |
|---------|-------------------|--------|--------|
| Monitoring coverage | ~2 km (MVP) | Full 11.35 km | Complete picture of dam health |
| Seepage monitoring | Monthly manual with gaps | 15-min automated + ML anomaly detection | Early warning days-to-weeks ahead |
| Compliance | Manual, paper-based, incomplete | Automated, 100% Dam Safety Act compliant | First digital compliance in Maharashtra |
| Inflow forecast | None (reactive) | 85%+ accuracy (3-day) | Proactive flood management possible |

## 3. Phase 3 Outcomes (Expansion)

| Outcome | Baseline | Target | Impact |

| Gosikhurd irrigated area | ~35,000 ha (14%) | 50,000+ ha (with canal module) | ~50,000 additional farmers benefit |

## 4. ROI for the Dam Operator (VIDC)

### Cost of Doing Nothing

| Consequence | Estimated Cost |
|-------------|---------------|
| Dam Safety Act non-compliance penalties | Fines + imprisonment risk for responsible officers |
| Building an in-house digital monitoring team | INR 2-5 Cr/year (staff + infrastructure) |
| Dam failure event (if unmonitored) | INR 100-500 Cr+ (downstream damage, liability, loss of life) |
| Continued irrigation inefficiency (Gosikhurd) | INR 60 Cr/year in unrealized crop value |

### Cost of Our Platform

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Annual SaaS subscription | INR 25-50 lakh/year |
| Sensor CapEx (one-time, can be DRIP-funded) | INR 40-60 lakh for full dam |

**ROI:** Even at the upper end (INR 50 lakh/year), our platform costs less than one junior engineer's salary — while providing 24/7 automated monitoring and compliance that an engineer with a notebook can't match.

## 5. ROI for Our Startup

| Milestone | Revenue | Cost to Achieve |
|-----------|---------|----------------|
| First paid contract (1 dam) | INR 25-50 lakh/year | INR ~3 Cr total investment |
| 10 dams (Year 3) | INR 3-5 Cr/year | Series A funded expansion |
| 50 dams (Year 5) | INR 15-25 Cr/year | 60-70% gross margins |

**Path to profitability:** ~15-20 dams at INR 35 lakh/dam/year = INR 5-7 Cr/year revenue, sufficient to cover a 20-person team. Achievable by Year 3-4.

## 6. Social Impact (For Grant/Impact Investor Narrative)

- **Farmer distress reduction:** Vidarbha has India's highest farmer suicide rate; improved irrigation directly addresses water scarcity
- **Lives protected:** Real-time monitoring can prevent dam failures (36 incidents in 2017-2024)
- **Water saved:** 20-25% reduction in canal losses = hundreds of MCM of water reaching farmers
- **Institutional capacity:** Government engineers gain digital skills, reducing dependence on external consultants
- **Replicable model:** Success at one dam creates a playbook for 5,334 others

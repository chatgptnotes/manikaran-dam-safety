## Stage 2: Seed Round (INR 1-2 Cr)

**Purpose:** Fund Phase 1 — hire core team (5-6 people), procure sensors, deploy MVP at Gosikhurd.

**Timing:** Close by Month 6 (before sensor procurement for pre-monsoon installation).

| **Avaana Capital** | Medium | INR 1-3 Cr | Sustainability/climate focus |
| **Ankur Capital** | Medium | INR 50L-2 Cr | Deeptech, climate, agriculture |
| **Aavishkaar** | Medium | INR 50L-2 Cr | Impact investor; Vidarbha farmer narrative resonates |
| **Villgro** | Medium | INR 25-50L + incubation | Early-stage impact; may lead to larger round |
| **Angel investors** | High | INR 10-50L each | 3-5 angels from water/infra/gov-tech backgrounds |
| **Incubators** | Medium-High | INR 10-25L + mentorship | T-Hub, SINE (IIT Bombay), NSRCEL (IIM-B), iCreate |

### Seed Pitch Essentials

1. **Problem:** 5,334 dams legally required to have monitoring; <15% do
2. **Solution:** Affordable sensor package + cloud dashboard + automated compliance reports
3. **Why now:** Dam Safety Act 2021 enforcement accelerating; DRIP funding active
4. **MVP:** 20 sensors on Gosikhurd for INR 15 lakh — full demo in 6 months
5. **TAM:** INR 600+ Cr instrumentation market; INR 25-50L/dam/year recurring SaaS
6. **Ask:** INR 1-2 Cr for 12-month runway to deploy, capture monsoon data, prove PMF

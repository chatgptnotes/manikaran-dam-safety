Pillar 2

Irrigation Canal Optimization

India's canals lose 35-45% of water before it reaches farmers. Our platform uses real-time flow sensors and AI to detect losses, optimize scheduling, and ensure equitable distribution from head to tail.

  • ✓ Real-time flow measurement at canal off-takes
  • ✓ Water mass-balance analysis (theft and leak detection)
  • ✓ Crop water demand estimation via satellite NDVI + weather
  • ✓ Dynamic canal scheduling replacing static Warabandi
  • ✓ Head-reach vs. tail-end equity monitoring
  • ✓ Soil moisture integration for precision irrigation

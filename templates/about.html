About JalRaksha

Making India's Dams Safe, One Sensor at a Time

We exist because India's 5,334 large dams deserve better than quarterly inspections with a notebook.

Our Mission

Affordable Dam Safety for Every State

The Dam Safety Act 2021 mandates real-time monitoring for every large dam in India. But most state dam operators lack the technical capability and budget to comply. Traditional instrumentation costs crores per dam — putting compliance out of reach.

We break that cost barrier. By combining smart sensor sourcing, lightweight IoT connectivity, and AI-powered automation, we make per-dam monitoring affordable for the first time — so no dam operator has to choose between compliance and their budget.

Our Approach

Start Small. Prove Value. Scale.

We don't ask you to instrument your entire dam on day one. We start with a critical section, demonstrate value, and expand based on results.

Phase 1

Deploy on One Critical Section

Install 15-20 sensors on the highest-risk section of your dam. Get real-time data flowing to the dashboard within weeks.

Phase 2

Capture First Monsoon Season

The monsoon is the ultimate stress test for any dam. We capture the full July-October dataset — the foundation for AI model training.

Phase 3

AI Models Go Live

With 12+ months of data, our anomaly detection and inflow forecasting models activate — adding predictive intelligence to your monitoring.

Phase 4

Expand & Automate Compliance

Scale sensor coverage across the full dam. Automated Dam Safety Act reports flow directly to NDSA/SDSO portals.

IoT Sensor Network

Industrial-Grade Geotechnical Instrumentation

Proven sensor technologies deployed in dams worldwide, optimized for Indian conditions — monsoon-rated, solar-powered, wirelessly connected.

💧

Pore Pressure Monitoring

Vibrating wire piezometers embedded in the dam body measure water pressure at multiple elevations — the single most important indicator of embankment health.

  • ✓ 0.025% F.S. accuracy
  • ✓ 20+ year operational life
  • ✓ Stainless steel, IP68 rated
🔍

Structural Movement

Crack meters, MEMS tiltmeters, and inclinometers detect millimeter-level displacement and angular changes in dam structures.

  • ✓ 0.001° tilt resolution
  • ✓ 0.01 mm displacement accuracy
  • ✓ Three-axis measurement
🌊

Seepage & Flow

Ultrasonic flow meters at toe drains quantify seepage volume. Combined with turbidity sensors, they detect internal erosion before it becomes visible.

  • ✓ Non-contact measurement
  • ✓ ±1% flow accuracy
  • ✓ Turbidity correlation
📶

LoRaWAN Connectivity

Long-range, low-power wireless network covering up to 15 km of dam length. Solar-powered gateways with 4G backhaul — works where there is zero infrastructure.

  • ✓ 10-15 km range
  • ✓ 7-10 year sensor battery life
  • ✓ AES-128 encryption
🛰

Satellite Monitoring (InSAR)

Sub-millimeter surface deformation detection from space using Sentinel-1 and commercial SAR satellites — covering the entire dam surface without any ground equipment.

  • ✓ Sub-mm deformation accuracy
  • ✓ Monthly full-dam coverage
  • ✓ No on-site hardware needed
🚁

Drone + AI Inspection

Quarterly LiDAR and photogrammetry surveys with AI-powered crack detection. Creates high-resolution 3D models of dam faces and spillway aprons.

  • ✓ cm-level 3D resolution
  • ✓ AI crack detection (CNN)
  • ✓ Sedimentation mapping
AI/ML Engine

Intelligence That Prevents Disasters

Our models learn from every monsoon season, getting smarter over time at predicting anomalies and optimizing water management.

Seepage Anomaly Detection

LSTM autoencoder trained on seasonal baselines. Detects rising pore pressure trends, accelerating creep, and abnormal seepage days to weeks before they become critical.

ParameterSpecification
ArchitectureIsolation Forest + LSTM Autoencoder
InputPiezometer readings, reservoir level, temperature, rainfall
OutputAnomaly score (0-1) with root-cause attribution
Detection Lead TimeDays to weeks before critical threshold

Inflow Forecasting

7-day hourly inflow prediction using LSTM with temporal attention, fed by IMD gridded rainfall, GFS/ECMWF ensemble weather models, and catchment soil moisture.

ParameterSpecification
ArchitectureLSTM + Temporal Attention
InputIMD rainfall, GFS/ECMWF forecasts, soil moisture
Forecast Horizon7 days (hourly resolution)
Target Accuracy85%+ (3-day), 70%+ (7-day)
Compliance & Standards

Built for Indian Regulatory Requirements

Dam Safety Act 2021

Automated report generation in NDSA-prescribed formats: pre-monsoon, post-monsoon, annual, and 5-year comprehensive reviews.

IS:7436 Compliance

All instrumentation follows Indian Standard IS:7436 for dam monitoring. Sensors calibrated against NABL-traceable reference standards.

CWC Data Formats

Direct integration with Central Water Commission data sheets, India-WRIS API, and WaterML 2.0 (OGC) for hydrological data exchange.

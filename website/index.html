The Problem
India's Dams Are Flying Blind

The Dam Safety Act 2021 mandates real-time instrumentation and compliance reporting, but most dam operators lack the digital infrastructure to comply.

No Real-Time Monitoring

Over 1,100 dams exceed their 50-year design life with manual inspection rounds at best. Sensors are absent or non-functional.

Compliance Burden

Dam Safety Act 2021 requires NDSA/SDSO-format reports. State operators face penalties including imprisonment for non-compliance.

No Affordable Solution Exists

Current providers offer expensive enterprise SCADA setups. No company offers compliance-as-a-service designed for India's dam operators.

Our Solution
IoT + AI + Automated Compliance

Install low-cost sensors, stream data to the cloud, detect anomalies automatically, and generate compliance reports in one click.

1

Install Sensors

Piezometers, crack meters, tiltmeters connected via LoRaWAN. Zero civil work. 1 day per section.

2

Stream to Cloud

Real-time data ingestion via LoRaWAN or 4G. Edge processing for local alarms. No internet dependency for core safety.

3

Detect Anomalies

Threshold alerts, rate-of-change detection, and ML-based trend analysis. Alerts via SMS, WhatsApp, and dashboard.

4

Auto Compliance Reports

One-click PDF reports in NDSA/SDSO-mandated formats. Monthly, quarterly, annual. Fully automated.
